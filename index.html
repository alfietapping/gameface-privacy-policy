Privacy Policy for GameFace

Last updated: June 12, 2025

1. Information We Collect

GameFace processes only one piece of data: a photo you upload. This is used solely to generate your footballer transformation.

2. How Your Photo is Used

Your image is temporarily sent to two third-party APIs for processing:

  • OpenAI API
  • LightX API

These services help us perform the transformation effect. The photo is not stored or used for any other purpose.

3. Data Retention & Deletion

Your photo is never stored. It is deleted immediately after processing.

4. Third-Party Services

We use OpenAI and LightX. Their privacy policies can be found here:

  • OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • LightX Privacy Policy

5. Children’s Privacy

GameFace is not for users under 13. If we receive a child’s photo, we delete it immediately.
